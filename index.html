portfolio.— a brief introduction

antoni "quei" żabicki is a 17-year-old digital creator, music producer, and graphic designer based in poznań, poland. active since 2021, his work seamlessly bridges the gap between sound and visual design. his portfolio spans both individual projects and collaborative ventures, taking full or partial responsibility for concept, execution, and strategic development. he won a prominent remix contest, leading to a release on cyanara records and chapter eight. currently, he is a co-owner of the tribbe project alongside tribbs, and independently runs the what-is-next™ label—a platform dedicated to providing tools, ideas, and ableton racks to help artists accelerate sums up their workflow. his signature sound is characterized by a unique fusion of polish music elements with modern edm, continually expanding into international records.
